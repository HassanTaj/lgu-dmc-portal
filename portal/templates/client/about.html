About Us

Detail Marks Certificate (DMC) is the portal all about students of educational institution to save their time in viewing their results manually. It also saves time and resources of exam department of an institute. This web portal is supported to remove and in some cases minimize the hardships faced by current system. This web portal is completely made on administrative. The administrator will allow or deny access to the portal. This web portal is to minimize manual work for entering and viewing students result certificate. The system will show the complete certificate of student result of class or semester. The information store in this way is also secure and safe. This web portal also increases the efficiency to manage studentâ€™s results. This web portal also helps the users to make online queries through online chat support. Our online chat support is available 24 hours to help users that how they use this portal.
